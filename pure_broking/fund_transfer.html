Fund Transfer

Remember to transfer funds only from the bank account registered with us

As per SEBI regulations, you have to maintain separate balances for Equities and Commodities accounts, so we have two different bank accounts to facilitate this. There are three different modes to deposit funds to your account.

UPI

You can transfer funds directly from the trading platform (Pure-TradeOn) using the instant* UPI. This comes at a charge of Rs. 10 plus taxes with the convenience of an instant credit.

Instant payment gateway

You can transfer funds directly from the trading platform (Pure-TradeOn) using the instant* payment gateway facility. This comes at a charge of Rs. 10 plus taxes with the convenience of an instant credit. We have about 28 banks (listed below) for this facility.
